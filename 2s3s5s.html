Place digits in the smallest area you can to make all of the goal products. Easy to play, hard to master. You'll put your puzzle skills to the test and enjoy numbers in a new way. It isn't easy to get the bonus, but when you do, you'll be determined to figure out how you did it!

Features include 4 difficulty levels, auto prime factorization, in-game calculator, and tips.
